⚙️ How It Works

From Input to Insights

Our ML pipeline processes each complaint through multiple analysis layers.

1

Text Ingestion & Tokenization

Complaint text is received and tokenized — split into individual terms. Stop words are identified and intensity modifiers (negators, amplifiers) are tracked.

2

Sentiment Analysis (NLP)

Each token is scored against a sentiment lexicon. Negators and intensifiers adjust polarity scores to produce a final Positive / Negative / Neutral label with confidence.

3

Text Classification

Domain keyword matching across 6 complaint categories using weighted TF-style scoring. The best-matching category is selected with a confidence percentage.

4

Priority Scoring

Urgency signals, emotional intensity (exclamation marks, length), and negative sentiment combine into a composite priority score: Low, Medium, or High.

5

Dashboard & Insights

All results are stored locally and aggregated into real-time charts, trend visualizations, and analytics summaries on the dashboard.
